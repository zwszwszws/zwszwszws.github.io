Short Biography I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science & Technology, University of Science and Technology Beijing. I received my PhD degree from Institute of Information System & Engineering, School of Software, Tsinghua University, where I was co-advised by Prof. Xiaojun Ye and Prof. Chaokun Wang. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree and master degree from Beijing JiaoTong University, advised by Prof. Yao Zhao.
